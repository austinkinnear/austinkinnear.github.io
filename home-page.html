Welcome to my website!

As a student-athlete studying computer science and playing men's volleyball at San Diego Mesa College, I am excited to pursue a career in software engineering and use my skills to make a positive impact on society. My experience with HTML, CSS, and Java includes building dozens of websites, including a mock website for the Save the Elephants foundation, as well as working on a current project called Look Out. I have also developed a lottery machine application using JavaFX. Through my experience as a team captain for four years in volleyball, I have developed excellent communication, organization, and leadership skills that are valuable in any team setting. These skills will undoubtedly translate well into a professional environment. Furthermore, I have participated in the youth USAV Hp program, where I was able to connect with other high-level volleyball players from across the United States, learn from their experiences, and develop my own skills. I understand the importance of building connections and collaborating with others to achieve common goals. Overall, I am eager to apply my technical skills and teamwork abilities to internships and future job opportunities in software engineering

On this website, you can learn more about me, my work, and my blog.
